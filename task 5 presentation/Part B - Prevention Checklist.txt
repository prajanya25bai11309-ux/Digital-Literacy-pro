**Title: Stay Safe Online - Practical Checklist**

- Always double-check the sender's email, not just the name.
- If a link looks even slightly suspicious, don't click it.
- Don't trust emails that create urgency like "limited time offer" or "act now."
- Never share your passwords or OTPs, no matter how real the request looks.
- Before applying anywhere, search the company and verify from their official website.
- Avoid logging into accounts through links-go directly to the official site instead.
- **UPI Safety:** Never send money for job applications, internships, or "verification fees."
- **UPI Safety:** Always check the receiver's name before confirming any payment.
- Keep your devices updated with basic security (antivirus, system updates).
- If something feels off, trust your instinct and stop right there.

**Report cybercrime immediately:**

- National Cyber Crime Portal: [https://cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in/)
- Helpline Number: **1930**
